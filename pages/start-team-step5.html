Step 5 of 9

Secure Funding

Plan your budget and find the first sponsors, donations, and grant support your team needs.

Most teams need a few thousand dollars for robot parts, registration, tools, and travel.

Typical expenses

  • Robot parts and electronics: $1,200–2,000
  • Competition registration: $500–600
  • Tools and workspace supplies: $300–500
  • Travel and event fees: $400–1,000

Funding sources

  • School budget or STEM funds
  • Corporate and local business sponsorships
  • Parent donations and team fundraising
  • Community grants and STEM program support
