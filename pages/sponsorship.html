Sponsorship Inquiry Email

Subject: Partnership Opportunity - FTC Team [Team Number]

Dear [Business Name],

We are Team [Number] – [Team Name], a robotics team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge. We're reaching out because your company's values align with our mission to inspire students in STEM.

Our team needs support for:

  • Competition registration and travel
  • Robot parts and tools
  • Team development and training

In return, we offer:

  • Logo placement on robot and team materials
  • Social media recognition
  • Company mention in outreach events

Would you be interested in supporting our team? We'd love to discuss partnership opportunities.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Position]
[Contact Information]

Grant Application Template Sections

Project Summary (200 words)

Describe your team, mission, and what you're building this season.

Problem Statement (200 words)

Explain the financial challenges and why funding is critical.

Goals & Objectives (300 words)

Outline specific, measurable goals for the season.

Budget Justification (200 words)

Break down exactly how funds will be used with specific quotes/estimates.

Impact & Outcomes (300 words)

Describe how this funding will help students and your community.

Sustainability (100 words)

Explain your long-term funding strategy beyond this grant.

Pro Tips

  • Personalize each message - avoid mass-sent templates
  • Be specific about needs and dollar amounts
  • Highlight impact and student benefits, not just robot parts
  • Always follow up with gratitude
  • Keep sponsors updated throughout the season
  • Provide ROI - show them how their money helps
  • Build long-term relationships, not one-time asks
