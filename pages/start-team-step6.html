Step 6 of 9

Set Up a Workspace

Create a place where your team can build, store parts, and collaborate safely.

Choose a space that supports both hands-on work and planning meetings.

  • Building and testing area for robot assembly
  • Meeting space for strategy, design, and planning
  • Storage for parts, tools, and robot pieces
  • Computer workstations for programming and CAD

Common team spaces include school workshops, classrooms, gym storage rooms, and community maker spaces.
