Step 7 of 9

Get Equipment & Parts

Collect the core parts your team needs to build a competitive FTC robot.

Start with the essentials and add parts as your design evolves.

  • REV Robotics Control Hub or similar FTC-approved controller
  • DC motors, servos, and motor controllers
  • Sensors such as color, distance, and touch sensors
  • Aluminum extrusion, fasteners, and chassis components
  • Tools like drills, saws, hex keys, and wrenches
  • Computers for programming, CAD, and robot design

Check the Mechanical Parts Guide for more detailed recommendations.
