Step 4 of 9

Find a Coach / Mentor

Secure adult mentors who can support meetings, registration, and technical learning.

Coaches and mentors help keep the team organized and safe while students learn robotics together.

  • Supervise meetings, build sessions, and travel.
  • Provide technical guidance and encourage learning.
  • Manage registration, paperwork, and communications.
  • Help students develop teamwork and engineering skills.

Tip: Mentors do not need to be robotics experts; willingness to learn is more important.
