Step 3 of 9

Recruit Team Members

Build a balanced team with students who can cover programming, mechanical design, electronics, and outreach.

Recruit a mix of skills so your team can design, build, program, and share its story.

  • Programmers: Write TeleOp and autonomous code.
  • Mechanical builders: Design and assemble the robot.
  • Electrical specialists: Handle wiring, sensors, and power.
  • Business/outreach members: Fundraising, presentations, and community work.

Target size: 8-15 students for a strong and adaptable team.
