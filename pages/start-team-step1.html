Step 1 of 9

Find a Sponsor Organization

Your FTC team needs a sponsoring school, club, community group, or nonprofit to register and compete.

FTC teams need a responsible sponsor to provide oversight, facilities, and official registration support.

Typical sponsor options

  • School through a STEM, science, or technology department
  • Community center or recreation program
  • Boys & Girls Club or similar youth organization
  • Library or maker space with a STEM focus
  • Nonprofit organization that supports robotics or education

Action: Talk to a school administrator, club leader, or nonprofit coordinator about sponsoring your team.
